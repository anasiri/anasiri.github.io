Planet Boom

  • Tech Stack: Unity, C#, Adobe Photoshop

This was a five-person group project for the game design class in 2019. The objective was to create entertaining game mechanics and then implement the game. This game's central mechanic is gravity. The aim is to defend the Earth against minor planets, each of which has a distinct gravity type. Players shoot asteroids at invading planets to destroy them. They have the option of purchasing additional weapons and upgrading their defense system.
