SmartClass was a five-person group project for the IoT class in 2021, intending to create an intelligent device to increase security in university classrooms.

We had a couple of tasks to handle:
1) Detect humans from a camera and log them into the database.
2) Auto-lock and unlock the class door.
3) Check the attendance of teachers and students.
4) Develop an admin panel for the security team.

I worked on the project's hardware and artificial intelligence components.

  • Tech Stack: Python, PyTorch, OpenCV, C, Docker, Raspberry Pi, Arduino, MQTT
